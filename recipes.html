Blueberry Recipes

Over the years, friends and family have shared their favorite ways to enjoy our blueberries. Here are a few beloved classics:

Blueberry Muffins

  • 2 cups fresh blueberries
  • 2 cups flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup melted butter

Mix dry and wet separately, then combine and fold in blueberries. Bake at 375°F for 25 minutes.

Blueberry Crisp

  • 4 cups blueberries
  • 1/3 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1/3 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1/4 cup butter

Place blueberries and sugar in a dish. Mix topping and crumble over berries. Bake at 350°F for 35–40 minutes.
