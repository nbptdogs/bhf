About Us

Blueberry Hill Farm is a family-owned and operated farm in Mt. Washington, Massachusetts. Since 1941, we’ve welcomed visitors from across the Northeast to pick their own blueberries and enjoy the natural beauty of the Berkshires.

We’re committed to sustainable farming, family traditions, and the joy of summer blueberry picking. Whether it’s your first time or a return visit, we’re grateful to share this land and harvest with you.

We look forward to seeing you on the hill!
